Account Help

I can't view certain pages

Many pages on this site are restricted to volunteers only. If you are a volunteer, please login or ask the volunteer coordinator to create an account for you.

I forgot my password/I want to change my password

If you forgot your password or want to change your current one, ask the volunteer coordinator to make you a new one. They will be able to do this from their admin panel.

Cat Help

I accidentally deleted a document/photo/event that I still need

The volunteer coordinator should be able to manually retrieve this file from their admin panel. You will need to tell them the cat name and file name or event that you are trying to recover, and they will be able to un-delete that document/photo/event.

How do I delete a cat photo?

While logged in as a volunteer, you can click on the "Update Info" button by the cat's name and scroll down to the bottom of the page. Here, you can delete the photos you want to remove.
